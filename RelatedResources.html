Related Resources

Welcome to the Reimagine Trustworthy Autonomous System for Young People project website for the workshop session at the Institute of Safe Autonomy (ISA), Computer Science Department, University of York. On this page you will find links to the resources presented on the day and some interesting links which you may wish to investigate further.

A github page has been created from which all of the downloadable materials are available for download.

You can download the materials or go to the websites directly from the table below.
